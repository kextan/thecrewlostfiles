About The Lost Crew Files

The Lost Crew Files is a digital horror archive, inspired by mystery and found-footage legends. It documents the strange, unsettling, and unexplained events surrounding the disappearance at Shamy Camp, Colorado. The project is designed as a collaborative investigation, mixing found logs, cryptic clues, and community theories.

Our aim is to create an immersive, narrative-driven experience for those obsessed with conspiracy, horror, and puzzle-solving. As new information is recovered, it will be posted, analyzed, and examined by you—the community.

Disclaimer: Some content may be disturbing. This is a fictional archive, blending reality and horror for entertainment and speculative purposes.
